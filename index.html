About Us

A Lifelong Legacy

A family-owned and operated business incorporated in 1949. We handle all lines of Insurance Investigations and settlements. Our founder and former Chairman of the Board, Benjamin Acosta, Sr., founded the Insurance field in the mid-1950s, with the present name becoming known in 1948. His son, Joaquín Acosta, joined the family operation in 1972 and has presided over the company for three decades. Today, three of his sons, Joaquín Jr., Ricardo, and Gabriel, are third-generation Acosta's involved with the firm.

We have maintained and continue to operate under the same moral and ethical integrity that has characterized us for approximately eighty-five (85) years. During this time, we have had the privilege and honor to look after the interests of Insurers, Excess Insurers, as well as our customers – the Insureds. Benj Acosta has served the industry through the years, assisting with investigations throughout the Island, the continental United States, the Caribbean, in addition to Mexico, Central, and South America.

Benj. Acosta, Sr. (1911 – 2006)

Former Chairman and Senior General Adjuster and Appraiser. Studied Civil Engineering and Liberal Arts in continental United States during the early 1930 decade. The Puerto Rico Branch Office handled Fire and Allied Lines, Casualty, Bonds, Marine Cargo and Hull losses throughout the Caribbean, Central and South America.
